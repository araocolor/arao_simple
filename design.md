# Design System

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.

## Brand Colors

Our brand utilizes a vibrant and distinct color palette:

* **Primary Color:** The most dominant brand color, used for key interactive elements and calls to action. We use `#2F88FB`.

* **Secondary Color:** A supportive color that complements the primary, used for less prominent UI elements and secondary actions. We use `#FF4D8D`.

* **Tertiary Color:** An accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements. We use `#0A101C`.

* **Neutral Color:** A foundational color for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements. We use `#64748B`.

## Typography

We define distinct font families for different textual hierarchies to ensure readability and brand consistency:

* **Headlines:** `plusJakartaSans` provides a modern and impactful feel for titles and headings.

* **Body Text:** `inter` is chosen for its excellent readability across various screen sizes and contexts.

* **Labels:** `inter` is also used for labels to maintain consistency with body text and ensure clarity.

## Shape and Form

Our UI elements are designed with a standard level of roundedness.

* **Roundedness:** A value of `2` indicates a moderate amount of rounding, balancing softness with a contemporary aesthetic.

## Spacing

The spatial relationships between UI elements are carefully considered to optimize user experience.

* **Spacing:** A value of `2` provides a normal, comfortable amount of whitespace, ensuring elements are neither too dense nor too spread out.

## Color Mode

The design system currently operates in a `light` color mode, optimized for bright environments.
